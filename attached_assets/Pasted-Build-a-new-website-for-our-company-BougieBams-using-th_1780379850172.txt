Build a new website for our company, BougieBams, using the project files provided as the primary source of truth.

## Project Overview

BougieBams is a premium Mahjong lifestyle brand focused on stylish, high-quality Mahjong products and accessories. The website should position the company as a luxury, modern, and aspirational brand while remaining approachable and easy to shop.

The primary goal of the website is to:

1. Sell Mahjong products directly to consumers.
2. Build brand awareness.
3. Grow an email subscriber list.
4. Showcase the unique BougieBams brand identity.
5. Create a foundation that can scale as new products are introduced.

The brand should feel:

* Premium
* Elegant
* Fun
* Modern
* Sophisticated

* Generic

## Design Direction

The website should have a luxury lifestyle feel similar to premium direct-to-consumer brands.

Focus on:

* Large photography
* Strong typography
* Generous whitespace
* Smooth animations
* Mobile-first design
* Fast page loads
* Call on the ui-us-pro-max skill to help with design direction

The design should feel comparable to luxury lifestyle and boutique retail brands rather than traditional gaming websites.
